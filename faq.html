The Turbo-Back means the sport exhaust system from the turbo to the tail pipes.

In general, the sport exhaust system should be cleaned regularly. However, all-weather drivers should pay particular attention to this, especially after the sport exhaust system has been exposed to road salt. Avoid contact with fuel and oil, specifically when the exhaust system is hot.
of a stainless steel sport exhaust system (black & matt)

  • Clean the exhaust system only after it has cooled down.
  • First clean|rinse the exhaust system with water.
  • Use a soft, damp, lint-free cloth to clean and remove water residues.
  • Do not use chemical cleaning agents.
  • Never use a mechanical or hard brush.

    • cleaning of a chrome sports exhaust system

    • Clean the exhaust system only after it has cooled down.
    • First clean|rinse the exhaust system with water.
    • Use a special cleaner for chrome.
    • Use a soft, damp, lint-free cloth to clean and remove water residues.
    • Never use a mechanical or hard brush.

    NOTE

    • Never clean the exhaust system when it is hot.
    • Do not use a high-pressure cleaner under any circumstances.
    • Do not use cleaning agents containing solvents - this can lead to discolorations|stains.
